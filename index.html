Games:


Up

Instructions: Use the mouse to rotate your ship - the ship will face your mouse pointer. Click to shoot. Kill all the enemies before they cross the line!

Oof

Instructions: Use WASD to move around in all directions. Use the arrow keys to shoot in any direction. If you hit another player (computer controlled), with your bullet, then it will turn blue and stop moving. If you are hit, you lose. Enemies will keep spawning.

Down

Instructions: Use the arrow keys to collect the glowing coins and go through the holes. You have a hundred seconds to get to the last hole. If you don't, you lose. The points are the amount of coins you collected, the time is how much time you have left, and your score is the time plus the points.
